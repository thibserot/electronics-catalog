# Mini toggle switch (MTS‑102/103) - SW003

Panel‑mount miniature SPDT toggle switches. Latching action. Variants: ON‑ON (MTS‑102) and ON‑OFF‑ON (MTS‑103). Good for power/select functions and mode switches.

## Links

- **Where to buy:** [AliExpress](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005006881432519.html)

## Specifications

- Contact: SPDT (3 pins)

- Electrical rating: 6A @ 125VAC, 3A @ 250VAC (low‑voltage DC suitable—derate)

- Bushing thread ≈ M6 (Ø ~6 mm), body ~13×8×10 mm

- Pack: 10 pcs


## Pinout & Addresses (common breakout labels)

- 3 pins: center is common (COM), sides are throws (A/B).

- ON‑ON: COM connects to A or B. ON‑OFF‑ON: COM connects to A, open, or B.


## Wiring

- For ESP32 logic input, wire COM to GPIO with pull‑up, throws to GND/3V3 to select levels, or route COM to your signal and A/B to rails.

- For switching power, place the load in series with COM and the chosen throw; mind current ratings.
