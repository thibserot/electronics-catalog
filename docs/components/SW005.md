# Pushbutton w/ LED (Self Locking) - SW005

16 mm metal pushbutton with illuminated LED (multi‑color options). Available in self‑locking (latching) and momentary variants. Good for power buttons and status‑lit inputs.

## Links

- **Where to buy:** [AliExpress](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005008682139922.html)

## Specifications

- Mount hole: 16 mm

- Action: Latching (self‑locking) or momentary (depends on variant)

- Contacts: typically SPST NO (check listing/part code)

- LED: separate two‑lead LED, supply per color spec (often 3V–12V with resistor)

- Terminals: 4+ pins (switch + LED pair)


## Pinout & Addresses (common breakout labels)

- Common layout: 2 switch terminals (NO) + 2 LED terminals (anode/cathode).

- Exact pin marks vary (e.g., “NO/COM” and “+/–” for LED); verify on the specific part.


## Wiring

- Switch: one terminal to ESP32 GPIO (INPUT_PULLUP), the other to GND.

- LED: drive via resistor from 3V3 (or use a transistor if drawing more current). Mind LED polarity.
